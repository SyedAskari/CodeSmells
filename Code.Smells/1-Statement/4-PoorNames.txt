SMELL : POOR NAMES
TYPE  : OBFUSCATOR

DEFINITION
Naming things has often been cited as one of the hardest 
problems in computer science. Use descriptive names and 
avoid abbreviations and encodings where possible.
The more long lived a particular variable is,
the more descriptive your name for it needs to be.

SOLUTION
Descriptive
Appropriate abstraction level
Follow standards
Unambiguous
Longer names for longer scopes
Don't encode or abbreviate
